The Wee Free Men

Nov 30, 2024

A young witch-in-training teams up with chaotic, pint-sized warriors to save her brother and face the Fairy Queen in this witty and enchanting tale.

Terry Pratchetts The Wee Free Men is a delightful and humorous fantasy novel that introduces readers to Tiffany Aching, a young witch-in-training with a sharp mind and a no-nonsense attitude. The story begins when Tiffany’s younger brother is kidnapped by the Queen of the Fairies, and she must rely on her wit and courage to rescue him.
